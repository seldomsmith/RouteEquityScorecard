Route Equity Index

The Route Equity Index (REI) is a comprehensive 0-100 score that quantifies the social utility of transit corridors. Driven by our proprietary Fair Share Algorithm and audited via SHAP glass-box inspectors, it transforms raw GTFS data into actionable civil rights insights.
